BerryDirect is a global supply chain service firm. We provide end-to-end solutions for global sourcing, fulfillment, distribution, freight, and storage. We serve emerging franchise brands as well as e-commerce, retail, and manufacturing companies.
We are committed to making our clients’ supply chains more efficient through affordable and reliable global sourcing, premium fulfillment services, and high-quality products.

Our History

In 1999, BerryDirect started sourcing and supplying decorative containers, boxes, equipment, and other customized, high-quality products for worldwide clients, including premier franchise brand Edible Arrangements. Since then, we have grown hand-in-hand with our clients and gained an invaluable understanding of their supply chain demands and how to best satisfy them through affordability, prompt deliveries, and constant communication. We have established an international network of teams and facilities in North America, Europe, and Asia that helps provide a seamless and dependable service to our many valued clients.
